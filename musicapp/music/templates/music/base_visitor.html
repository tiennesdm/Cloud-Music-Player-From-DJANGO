About Cloud Music Player

Cloud Music Player is an app that allows you to upload and store all of your music on the cloud.

How do I add music?

First, Select the option create a new album by filling out the form on this page. Once an album is created you will be able to add/upload songs.

What are some Album logo best practices?

  • Have a resolution of 512x512
  • Use common image formats such as .JPG, .GIF, or .PNG
  • Remain under the 2MB limit.
  • Square images look best
